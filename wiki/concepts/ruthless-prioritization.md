# Ruthless Prioritization

**Definition:** The systematic elimination of lower-value commitments to create protected time and cognitive bandwidth for the single highest-leverage pursuit.

## Why It Matters

Peak performers don't just work hard — they ruthlessly cut everything that isn't mission-critical. Divided attention is the enemy of mastery: the brain cannot achieve deep learning or flow while context-switching across competing commitments. Kotler presents ruthless prioritization not as a productivity hack but as a prerequisite for impossible goals. Without it, the motivation stack never builds enough momentum to produce flow at the highest levels.

## Key Principles

- **MTP as filter:** The Massively Transformative Purpose determines what stays and what goes
- **Attention is finite:** Every yes is a no to something else; ruthless prioritization makes this explicit
- **Mastery requires depth:** Deep skill acquisition is incompatible with chronic context-switching
- **The math of life:** Calculate finite opportunities; not everything can be done before time runs out
- **Q2 first:** Schedule your most important work before the urgent fills the calendar
- **Creative procrastination:** Intentionally delay D/E tasks to protect time for A tasks
