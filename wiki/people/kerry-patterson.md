# Kerry Patterson

**Role:** Lead author of *Crucial Conversations* and co-founder of VitalSmarts (now Crucial Learning). Organizational behavior researcher and corporate training pioneer.

## Why He Matters

Patterson and his co-authors (Joseph Grenny, Ron McMillan, Al Switzler) spent 25 years studying people who were consistently effective in high-stakes dialogue. Their research produced the Crucial Conversations framework — a practical, trainable system for staying in productive dialogue when opinions vary, stakes are high, and emotions run strong.

## Key Contributions

| Contribution | Description |
|---|---|
| Pool of Shared Meaning | The larger the shared pool of information between dialogue partners, the better the decision and the stronger the commitment. |
| Silence & Violence taxonomy | Two failure modes in dialogue: withdrawal/avoidance (silence) or controlling/attacking (violence). |
| STATE My Path | Share facts → Tell your story → Ask for others' paths → Talk tentatively → Encourage testing. |
| AMPP Listening | Ask → Mirror → Paraphrase → Prime — a deep listening protocol. |
| Three Clever Stories | Victim, Villain, Helpless — the self-deception moves people make mid-conversation. |
