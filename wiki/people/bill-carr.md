# Bill Carr

**Role:** Former VP of Digital Media at Amazon; led the teams that built Amazon Music and Amazon Video; co-author of *Working Backwards* (2021) with Colin Bryar. Expert in Amazon's two-pizza team model and single-threaded ownership.

---

## Why He Matters to This Wiki

Carr led some of Amazon's most visible consumer product launches and had a front-row seat to how Amazon's operating mechanisms work — and fail — at scale. His perspective on Amazon Studios (where Working Backwards discipline broke down) is one of the book's most instructive case studies.

---

## Key Contributions

- **Two-pizza teams and single-threaded ownership** — Practical implementation of small, focused teams with unambiguous accountability.
- **Amazon Digital Media build-out** — Illustrates how PR-FAQ and Working Backwards work for complex, multi-year product bets.
