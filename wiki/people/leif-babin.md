# Leif Babin

**Role:** Retired U.S. Navy SEAL officer; served under Jocko Willink in Task Unit Bruiser during the Battle of Ramadi (Iraq, 2006); co-author of *Extreme Ownership* (2015) and co-founder of Echelon Front leadership consultancy.

---

## Why He Matters to This Wiki

Babin provides the ground-level SEAL perspective in *Extreme Ownership* — his battlefield stories bring specific principles to life with vivid tactical context. Where Willink often provides the strategic overlay, Babin's chapters tend to center on subordinate-leader challenges: leading up the chain, managing an ego under fire, implementing Decentralized Command from below.

---

## Key Contributions

- **Leading up the chain** — How subordinate leaders manage their relationship with senior leaders effectively. Own the communication gap; never let your boss be surprised.
- **Check the Ego** — Babin's chapters frequently illustrate how ego is the invisible obstacle in near-misses and leadership failures.
