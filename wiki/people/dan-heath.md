# Dan Heath

**Role:** Co-author of *Decisive*, *Made to Stick*, *Switch*, and *The Power of Moments*. Senior Fellow at Duke University's CASE center (Center for the Advancement of Social Entrepreneurship). Researcher and practitioner in decision-making, behavior change, and social impact.

---

## Why He Matters to This Wiki

Dan Heath (with his brother Chip) co-built a practical library of frameworks for leaders navigating complex human problems — from making better decisions to driving change to creating memorable experiences. His work bridges academic research and executive practice.

---

## Key Contributions

- **WRAP Framework** — Four-step decision process. See [[wrap-framework]].
- **Switch (with Chip Heath)** — Change management through the Rider/Elephant/Path model. Rational vs. emotional drivers of behavior.
- **Made to Stick (with Chip Heath)** — The science of memorable, persuasive communication.
- **Upstream (solo)** — Moving from reactive problem-solving to proactive prevention. Relevant to systems thinking in leadership.
