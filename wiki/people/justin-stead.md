# Justin Stead

**Role:** Business leader and co-author of *The Stoic Leader*; brings practitioner perspective to Stoic leadership.

## Background

- Senior business executive and board-level operator
- Co-authored *The Stoic Leader* to apply Stoic principles in real organizational contexts
- Emphasizes practical execution, team stewardship, and response quality under pressure

## Why He Matters in This Wiki

Stead anchors Stoicism in modern leadership realities: uncertainty, pace, and accountability. His lens helps convert philosophy into concrete habits leaders can use daily.
