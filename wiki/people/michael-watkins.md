# Michael D. Watkins

**Role:** Co-founder of Genesis Advisers (leadership transition consultancy). Professor at IMD Business School (Switzerland). Previously on faculty at Harvard Business School, Harvard Kennedy School, and INSEAD. Leading authority on leadership transitions.

## Why He Matters to This Wiki

Watkins identified leadership transitions as a distinct discipline requiring specific skills — not just an extension of what you already know. Before his work, most leaders treated new roles as "do more of what worked before." His framework revealed that transitions are predictable, manageable, and (when handled well) powerful accelerators of career and organizational success.

## Key Contributions

- **[[transition-acceleration]]** — Treating transitions as a learnable discipline with specific strategies.
- **STARS Model** — Five business situations (Start-up, Turnaround, Accelerated growth, Realignment, Sustaining success) requiring different strategies.
- **[[early-wins]]** — Securing visible, meaningful results in the first 30–60 days.
- **Five Conversations with Your Boss** — Structured alignment on situation, expectations, resources, style, development.
- **Coalition Building** — Mapping allies, fence-sitters, and resistors to build influence strategically.
