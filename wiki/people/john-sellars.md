# John Sellars

**Role:** Philosopher and Stoicism scholar; co-author of *The Stoic Leader*.

## Background

- Academic philosopher focused on classical philosophy and practical Stoicism
- Associated with Modern Stoicism and university teaching/research roles in the UK
- Writes for both scholarly and practitioner audiences

## Why He Matters in This Wiki

Sellars helps translate Stoicism from historical doctrine into a practical leadership method. In this wiki, his contribution is the explicit bridge between philosophy and daily managerial behavior under uncertainty.
