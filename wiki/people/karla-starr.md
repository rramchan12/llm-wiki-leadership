# Karla Starr

**Role:** Science writer and researcher; co-author of *Making Numbers Count* (2022) with Chip Heath. Expert in translating scientific and data-driven concepts for general audiences.

---

## Why She Matters to This Wiki

Starr brings the science-writing and narrative craft dimension to Heath's behavioral-science frameworks. Where Heath provides the cognitive psychology, Starr brings the practical translation toolkit developed through years of explaining complex quantitative information accessibly.

---

## Key Contributions

- **Numeracy Translation** — Co-developed the concrete-comparison and human-scale-reduction toolkit for making abstract data felt. See [[numeracy-translation]].
- **The One-Person Rule** — Operationalizing the principle that individuals move audiences while statistics numb them.
