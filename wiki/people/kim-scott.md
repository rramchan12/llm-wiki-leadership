# Kim Scott

**Role:** Author of *Radical Candor*. Former executive at Google and Apple. CEO coach and co-founder of Radical Candor, Inc. Management advisor who has worked with companies including Twitter, Dropbox, and Qualtrics.

## Why She Matters to This Wiki

Scott provides the most practical and widely adopted framework for leadership feedback in this wiki. While Arbinger explains *why* honest communication fails (self-deception) and Crucial Conversations provides *mechanics* for high-stakes dialogue, Radical Candor provides the daily *management philosophy* that makes honest feedback the norm rather than the exception.

## Key Contributions

- **[[radical-candor]]** — The Care Personally × Challenge Directly 2×2 framework. Four quadrants: Radical Candor, Ruinous Empathy, Obnoxious Aggression, Manipulative Insincerity.
- **Feedback order of operations** — Solicit → Praise → Criticize. All specific, all sincere.
- **Rockstars vs. Superstars** — Two types of excellent performers who need fundamentally different management approaches.
- **GSD Wheel** — Collaborative decision-making cycle: Listen → Clarify → Debate → Decide → Persuade → Execute → Learn.
