# Colin Bryar

**Role:** Former VP at Amazon; served as Jeff Bezos's Chief of Staff ("shadow") for two years; co-author of *Working Backwards* (2021) with Bill Carr. Expert in Amazon's operating mechanisms and leadership principles.

---

## Why He Matters to This Wiki

Bryar spent over a decade as a senior Amazon executive, including two years inside Bezos's inner circle. He was present during the creation and refinement of Amazon's core operating mechanisms — the PR-FAQ, Bar Raiser process, six-page narrative, and Leadership Principles. *Working Backwards* decodes the "how" behind Amazon's operational culture at source.

---

## Key Contributions

- **Working Backwards / PR-FAQ** — Co-documented the method that inverts traditional product development. See [[working-backwards-pr-faq]].
- **Bar Raiser hiring** — Every hire raises the bar; a designated Bar Raiser has unilateral veto.
- **Leadership Principles as mechanisms** — The insight that values without enforcement mechanisms don't change behavior.
