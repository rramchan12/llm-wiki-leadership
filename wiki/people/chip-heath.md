# Chip Heath

**Role:** Co-author of *Decisive*, *Made to Stick*, *Switch*, and *The Power of Moments*. Professor at Stanford Graduate School of Business. Researcher in decision-making, organizational behavior, and the psychology of how ideas spread.

---

## Why He Matters to This Wiki

Chip Heath (with his brother Dan) built one of the most practically accessible bodies of work on how humans make decisions, communicate ideas, and change behavior. Their books translate behavioral economics and cognitive psychology into actionable frameworks for leaders and managers.

---

## Key Contributions

- **WRAP Framework** — Four-step decision process that counters the four cognitive villains of bad decisions. See [[wrap-framework]].
- **Made to Stick (with Dan Heath)** — Why some ideas survive and others die. The SUCCESS model for sticky communication: Simple, Unexpected, Concrete, Credentialed, Emotional, Story.
- **Switch (with Dan Heath)** — How to drive change when change is hard. The Rider/Elephant/Path model of rational vs. emotional decision-making.
- **The Power of Moments (with Dan Heath)** — Why certain experiences are disproportionately memorable and meaningful; how to design them intentionally.
